Challenge
Strategic Color Selection

The color for the app is directly related to the logo, but there was no logo for this app, so I had to create the logo myself at the CEO's request. They said that they want to use two colors for this app. They wanted to use blue as one of 2 colors and asked me to come up with a color that would go with the other blue. I chose green, and the purpose of choosing green is for the following purposes.

  • Green and blue are often associated with stability, trustworthiness, and reliability. In the context of currency exchange, where users are dealing with financial transactions and sensitive information, these colors can help instill confidence in the platform and its services.

  • Incorporating green and blue colors into currency exchange applications can help convey a sense of trust, professionalism, and stability while also facilitating clear communication and enhancing the overall user experience.

In this project, there are two mobile applications, and in order to differentiate the two applications in terms of consistency, blue is preferred for the admin app, and green is preferred for the user app.

Why was green used for the user app?

Green is commonly linked with growth, prosperity, and positivity, aligning well with the user app's purpose of facilitating currency transactions and fostering a positive user experience.

Money transactions

As a user, when a money transaction is done,we can check the tracking about each transcation step. This point is one of the stakeholder needs. I assume that it is an important feature for a reliable and secure currency exchange app.

Crafting a currency graph

The primary goal is to effectively visualize complex exchange rate data in a manner that is both visually appealing and easy to comprehend on mobile screens. This involves striking a balance between displaying detailed information and maintaining clarity and simplicity in the design.

Notification of transactions and account activities

Stakeholders wanted to add a function that alerts users of currency transactions and other important information. So, I designed clear and concise alert messages that convey relevant information at a glance, selecting appropriate visual cues. The goal is to strike a balance between providing valuable information to users without overwhelming them with unnecessary notifications, ultimately contributing to a seamless and efficient currency exchange experience.
